Story


We saw a big unfulfilled demand for excess inventory, surplus and seconds in a highly fragmented market. Suppliers are looking for a fast efficient way to sell their excess goods at fair price while buyers are looking for the latest and best quality products at the cheapest price. That’s why we created XSTOK!


XSTOK is a B2B auction platform to facilitate buying and selling of excess goods.


Our vision is to help our buyers and suppliers use technology to reduce inefficiencies and drive new businesses through an easy-to-use, always available auction platform.


We have started on this vision by focusing on the textile industry. Our mission is to provide our buyers and sellers a marketplace for hard-to-sell and slow moving stocks of apparels, home textiles, fabrics and yarns at market driven prices.


Our revolutionary auction based mobile platform has been certified by leading agencies, and is combined with critical services such as quality inspection, testing, banking, logistics and warehousing which will change the way business is done.
