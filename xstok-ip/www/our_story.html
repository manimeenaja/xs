The XSTOK Story

We started with a simple idea – buying and selling ex stock goods, including surplus should be easy. Buyers need access to high quality, in-demand products and suppliers need to sell goods at market driven prices. So begins the story of XSTOK ...

The story about a vision to provide buyers and sellers with a new way of doing business. A vision that in a click, sellers have access to a large universe of buyers and buyers have access to what they need, when they need it.

Thus evolved XSTOK – The XS stock marketplace!

A revolutionary B2B auction platform that functions as a central marketplace for buying and selling of ready and surplus goods.

Our marketplace is now open to the textile industry. We bring to the world, a variety of curated, popular and hard-to-find stocks of apparels, home textiles, fabrics and yarns.

With certifications from leading agencies and top-of-the-line services, such as quality inspection, banking, insurance and logistics, our story has now become the central point for greater business possibilities.
